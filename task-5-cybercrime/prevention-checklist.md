## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests  
- Do not share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone  
- Always verify the identity of the sender before making transactions  
- Use only official and trusted payment applications  
- Enable SMS and app notifications for transactions  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity  
- Immediately report any fraud on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
